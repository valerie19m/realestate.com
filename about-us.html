About Us

Our Company

JustHome is the first SINGLE STOP real estate agency in the Republic of Moldova that offers assistance and advice in the process of selling, buying or renting real estate - house, apartment, office, commercial / industrial space or land. Our experts from Chisinau and Balti will help you through the entire transaction process: from evaluation and examination, presentation and promotion, preparation of documents for a notarial transaction, to cadastre registration and tax inspection.
